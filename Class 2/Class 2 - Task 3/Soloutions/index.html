Ingredients

  • Bell Pepper
  • Red Onion
  • White Onion
  • Black beans
  • Fajita Spice Mix
  • Fajita Sauce
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Olive Oil

Recipe

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. While the pan heats, slice the bell pepper and the onions into small chunks.
  3. Drain the black beans.
  4. When the oil is heated add the veg mix and the spice mix to the pan and simmer for a few minutes until soft.
  5. Pour over the sauce and stir before allowing the mix to simmer for 5 - 10 minutes.
  6. While the mix simmers, separate the tortillas and place them under the grill at a very low heat for a few moments to heat them up.
  7. Place the filling in a large serving bowl and place on the table.
  8. Add desired number of tortillas to each plate and serve.
  9. Enjoy!
